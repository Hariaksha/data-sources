# Freedom in the World (Freedom House)

**Theme:** Governance & Democracy
**Source:** Freedom House
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (210 countries and territories)
**Time Coverage:** 1972 – present (annual)
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Downloadable Excel/CSV, interactive
**Access:** [Freedom House Country Scores](https://freedomhouse.org/country/scores)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Annual assessment of political rights and civil liberties. Countries are scored 1–7 on
political rights and civil liberties (lower = freer) and classified as Free, Partly Free,
or Not Free. One of the longest-running political freedom datasets available.

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **Democracy Index**, **CPI**, **HDI** via `iso3c`
- Widely used as a democracy/freedom control in cross-country regressions
