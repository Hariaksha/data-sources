# Democracy Index (EIU / Our World in Data)

**Theme:** Governance & Democracy
**Source:** Economist Intelligence Unit (via Our World in Data)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (167 countries)
**Time Coverage:** 2006 – present (annual)
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Interactive chart, downloadable
**Access:** [Democracy Index – Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/democracy-index-eiu)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Scores countries 0–10 across five dimensions: electoral process, government functioning,
political participation, political culture, and civil liberties. Classifies regimes as full
democracy, flawed democracy, hybrid regime, or authoritarian.

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **Freedom House**, **CPI**, **FSI** via `iso3c`
