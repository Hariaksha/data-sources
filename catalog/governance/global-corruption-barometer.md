# Global Corruption Barometer (GCB)

**Theme:** Governance
**Source:** Transparency International
**Geographic Coverage:** Selected countries (varies by edition)
**Time Coverage:** Periodic surveys
**Unit of Observation:** Individual (survey)
**Format:** Report, microdata (some editions)
**Access:** Link pending — check [Transparency International](https://www.transparency.org)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

A public opinion survey asking individuals about their direct experiences with bribery and
their views on corruption. Complements the CPI by capturing citizen-level experience rather
than expert perception.

## Notes

- Distinct from the CPI: this is survey-based, individual-level data
- Useful for micro-level corruption research or as a complement to CPI in mixed-methods work
