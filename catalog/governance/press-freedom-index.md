# World Press Freedom Index

**Theme:** Governance & Democracy
**Source:** Reporters Without Borders (RSF)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (180 countries)
**Time Coverage:** 2002 – present (annual)
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Interactive, downloadable
**Access:** [RSF World Press Freedom Index](https://rsf.org/en/index)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Annual ranking of press freedom conditions across five indicators: political context, legal
framework, economic context, sociocultural context, and safety of journalists.

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **Freedom House**, **Democracy Index** via `iso3c`
