# Global Militarization Index (GMI)

**Theme:** Conflict & Security
**Source:** BICC (Bonn International Centre for Conflict Studies)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global
**Time Coverage:** Annual
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Interactive, downloadable
**Access:** [gmi.bicc.de](https://gmi.bicc.de/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures the degree of militarization of a country relative to its population and economic
output — comparing military expenditure, personnel, and arms holdings to GDP and population.

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **GPI**, **FSI** via `iso3c`
