# Global Terrorism Index (GTI)

**Theme:** Conflict & Safety
**Source:** Institute for Economics & Peace (IEP)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global
**Time Coverage:** 2000 – present (annual)
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** PDF report, downloadable data
**Access:** [GTI – IEP](https://www.economicsandpeace.org/global-terrorism-index/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures the impact of terrorism across countries using incidents, fatalities, injuries,
and property damage. Complements the GPI with a terrorism-specific lens.

## Potential Research Questions

- Does terrorism intensity correlate with state fragility or climate stress?
- Useful as a control variable in conflict-development regressions

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **ACLED**, **GPI**, **Fragile States Index** via `iso3c`
