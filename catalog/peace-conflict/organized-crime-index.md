# Global Organized Crime Index

**Theme:** Conflict & Safety
**Source:** Global Initiative Against Transnational Organized Crime
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (193 countries)
**Time Coverage:** 2021, 2023 (biennial)
**Unit of Observation:** Country
**Format:** Interactive map, downloadable data
**Access:** [ocindex.net](https://ocindex.net/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Scores countries on criminality (across 10 criminal markets) and resilience (institutional,
social, and legal capacity to resist organized crime). Provides both a criminality score and
a resilience score per country.

## Potential Research Questions

- Does organized crime intensity predict state fragility or conflict onset?
- Intersection of criminal markets with resource extraction / environmental crime in Indonesia
