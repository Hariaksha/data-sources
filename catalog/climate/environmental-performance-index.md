# Environmental Performance Index (EPI)

**Theme:** Climate & Environment
**Source:** Yale Center for Environmental Law & Policy
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (180 countries)
**Time Coverage:** Biennial (2002 – present)
**Unit of Observation:** Country
**Format:** Downloadable CSV, interactive
**Access:** [EPI 2022 Results – Yale](https://epi.yale.edu/epi-results/2022/component/epi)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Ranks countries on environmental health and ecosystem vitality using 40 performance
indicators across 11 issue categories including air quality, water, biodiversity, climate
change, and waste management. One of the broadest environmental performance benchmarks
available.

## Potential Research Questions

- Does environmental performance predict climate-conflict vulnerability?
- Cross-country comparison of environmental governance in Asia-Pacific
