# Global Sustainable Competitiveness Index (GSCI)

**Theme:** Climate & Sustainable Development
**Source:** Solability
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (180 countries)
**Time Coverage:** Annual
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Interactive, downloadable
**Access:** [solability.com/the-global-sustainable-competitiveness-index](https://solability.com/the-global-sustainable-competitiveness-index)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures long-term sustainable competitiveness across five pillars: natural capital,
social cohesion, resource intensity, intellectual capital, and governance. Distinct from
purely environmental rankings by integrating economic and social sustainability dimensions.

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **EPI**, **HDI**, **Net Zero Tracker** via `iso3c`
