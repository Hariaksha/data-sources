# Social Institutions and Gender Index (SIGI)

**Theme:** Development & Gender Equality
**Source:** OECD Development Centre
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (~180 countries)
**Time Coverage:** Periodic (2009, 2012, 2014, 2019, 2023)
**Unit of Observation:** Country
**Format:** Downloadable, interactive
**Access:** [SIGI – OECD](https://www.oecd.org/en/about/programmes/social-institutions-and-gender-index-sigi.html)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures discriminatory social institutions — formal and informal laws, norms, and practices
that restrict women's rights and opportunities. Covers family code, restricted physical
integrity, restricted access to productive resources, and restricted civil liberties.
