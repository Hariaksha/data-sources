# Global Health Security Index (GHS Index)

**Theme:** Development & Health
**Source:** Johns Hopkins Center for Health Security / Nuclear Threat Initiative
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (195 countries)
**Time Coverage:** 2019, 2021 (biennial)
**Unit of Observation:** Country
**Format:** Downloadable, interactive
**Access:** [GHS Index](https://ghsindex.org/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Comprehensive assessment of health security preparedness across six categories: prevention,
detection, response, health system capacity, compliance with international norms, and risk
environment. Gained prominence during COVID-19 as a benchmark for pandemic preparedness.
