# World Happiness Report

**Theme:** Development & Wellbeing
**Source:** Sustainable Development Solutions Network (SDSN)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (~150 countries)
**Time Coverage:** 2012 – present (annual)
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** Downloadable CSV, PDF report
**Access:** [World Happiness Report](https://worldhappiness.report/about/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Ranks countries by self-reported life satisfaction (Cantril ladder score) from Gallup World
Poll data. Decomposes scores into contributions from GDP per capita, social support, healthy
life expectancy, freedom, generosity, and absence of corruption.

## Notes

- Underlying data comes from the **Gallup World Poll** — see `catalog/surveys/gallup-world-poll.md`
- The happiness score is essentially a Gallup microdata aggregate

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **HDI**, **GII**, **Freedom House** via `iso3c`
