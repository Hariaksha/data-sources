# Global Multidimensional Poverty Index (MPI)

**Theme:** Development
**Source:** UNDP / Oxford Poverty and Human Development Initiative (OPHI)
**Geographic Coverage:** ~110 developing countries
**Time Coverage:** Annual (recent years)
**Unit of Observation:** Country (and subnational in some editions)
**Format:** Downloadable, interactive
**Access:** [Global MPI 2025 – UNDP](https://hdr.undp.org/content/2025-global-multidimensional-poverty-index-mpi#/indicies/MPI)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures poverty across three dimensions (health, education, living standards) using 10
indicators. Captures deprivation not reflected in income-based poverty measures. Available
at subnational level for many countries, enabling within-country analysis.

## Potential Research Questions

- Does multidimensional poverty predict vulnerability to climate shocks?
- Subnational variation in Indonesia: do high-poverty regions experience more conflict?

## Merge Keys & Compatibility

- Merge with **HDI**, **FSI**, **CCVI** via `iso3c`
- Subnational data can link to ACLED admin-level data
