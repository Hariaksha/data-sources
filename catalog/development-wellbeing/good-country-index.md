# Good Country Index

**Theme:** Development & International Relations
**Source:** Simon Anholt / Good Country
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (163 countries)
**Time Coverage:** Periodic
**Unit of Observation:** Country
**Format:** Interactive, downloadable
**Access:** [index.goodcountry.org](https://index.goodcountry.org/)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Measures how much each country contributes to the global commons relative to its size,
across seven categories: science & technology, culture, international peace & security,
world order, planet & climate, prosperity & equality, and health & wellbeing.

## Potential Research Questions

- Which countries "punch above their weight" in global contributions?
- Useful for contextualizing bilateral influence and foreign policy research
