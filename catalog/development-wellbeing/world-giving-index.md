# World Giving Index

**Theme:** Development & Social Capital
**Source:** Charities Aid Foundation (CAF)
**Geographic Coverage:** Global (~140 countries)
**Time Coverage:** Annual
**Unit of Observation:** Country-year
**Format:** PDF report, downloadable data
**Access:** [CAF World Giving Index](https://www.cafonline.org/insights/research/world-giving-index)
**License:** Free to access
**Last Verified:** April 2026

---

## Description

Based on Gallup World Poll data, measures three dimensions of giving behavior: helping a
stranger, donating money, and volunteering time. Proxy for civic engagement and social
capital at the country level.
